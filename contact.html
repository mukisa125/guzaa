About

Gula Kuze Enterprise is a leading agribusiness dedicated to transforming Uganda’s agricultural sector. We specialize in growing high-quality crops such as Oil products, Organic products, fruits, Vegetables among others using sustainable farming practices. Our mission is to empower local farmers, promote food security.
